Getting to Nagano

Tokyo ↠ Nagano (train):

Tokyo and Nagano are connected by the station, Hokuriku Shinkansen. A one way trip is about 80-100 minutes for 8000 yen. The trip is fully covered by the Japan Rail Pass and the JR East Nagano Niigata Area Pass.

Tokyo ↠ Nagano (bus):

There are multiple bus companies operate frequent daytime highway buses between Tokyo and Nagano station. A one way trip is 3.5 hrs. Conventional bus companies charge about 4000 yen, while fares discount operator companies like Willer Express are about 2000 yen for one way. Online reservations can also be made through Willer and Japan Bus Online.

Bus companies operate daytime and nighttime highway buses between Osaka and Nagano like, Hankyu Umeda Highway Bus Terminal and OCAT Namba. A one way trip is about 8 hours and costs about 8000 yen. Online reservations can also be made through Willer and Japan Bus.
