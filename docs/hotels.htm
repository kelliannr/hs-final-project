Places to Stay

Aburaya Tousen

Aburaya Tosen is a 5 star hotel that is an elegant and modern japanese style lodging. It is 7 minutes from the Yudanaka station in Nagano, which allows for easy transportation access. The hotel has 11 different types of baths, including some rooms with open-air baths. The rooms are spacious and feature tatami maps, paper sliding doors, traditional futon beds and a seating area . As amenities, the hotel offers free wifi, a restaurant , a spa, hot springs with mountain views and a karaoke room. You can look forward to saunas, open air baths, massages and private baths. Tobira Onsen Myojikan

Tobira Onsen Myojikan is a 4.5 star hotel, located near the Tobira Onsen area. Tobira Onsen Myojikan provides shuttleservice, wifi availability and non-smoking rooms. It is also 30 minutes from Matsumoto castle and 60 minutes from Matsumoto airport so nearby sightseeing and transportation isclose. The japanese style rooms are furnished with large window and a carpeted seating area. The guest rooms include an air conditioning unit, a large living room with a sofa, a flat screen TV, a safety box and a private bathroom with a toothbrush and hair dryer set.
