Nagano's Weather

Nagano is one of the coldest regions in Japan with an average daily high temperature of only 18℃. It is cold, wet and a few beautiful summer months are also happening during a year. The best time of year to visit Nagano is May through September if you want good weather with comfortable temperatures. The wettest month is December, the driest is May, the warmest is August, and the coolest is March, so going in between will give the ideal experience. Nagano gets an annual average of about 2,000 hours of sun. There is a high rate of clear and cloudless skies and relatively low humidity, with an average temperature of 12℃ (57℉). Nagano has a dry climate, holding one of the lowest precipitation rates in Japan with the annual average about 1288mm (128.8cm, 1.3 m).
