Where to Eat

Ramen Zunpachi

Ramen Zunpachi uses a family recipe, making the restaurant’s food unique. They are known for their signature tonkatsu shoyu ramen which combines pork bone broth and soy sauce in silky soup with thick homemade noodles. Another favorite is the miso ramen which has a satisfyingly sweet and creamy blend of red and white Shinshu miso. Dinner is 990 yen (8.93 US) and lunch is the same price.

Chushin Kaikan RYU-TAN

Chushin Kaikan RYU-TAN serves authentic chinese food in an elegant, yet comfortable atmosphere. Ingredients are painstakingly chosen and treated with care, resulting in great flavor. The chefs aim to serve food that looks beautiful so they plate their dishes creatively. The restaurant can also host parties and celebrations where a private room would be used. Dinner is 1,000 yen (9.27 US) and lunch is 800 yen (7.42 US).

Tempura Maki

Tempura Maki is located in Karuzawa, Nagano and has been in operation for 55 years. It is open every April to November. It serves the area’s finest tempura, using seasonal vegetables that vary based on the time of year. Chefs make tempura one by one, paying attention to temperature for the most crispness and flavor. Tempura Maki is the place to go for a traditional japanese taste. Dinner is 3,800 yen (33.83 US) and lunch is 1,600 yen (14.25 US).
